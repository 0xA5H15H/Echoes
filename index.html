How Echoes Works

Discover

See what's happening around you — genuine activities and events nearby. A feed that favors presence, not performance.

Join or Create

Jump into Echoes forming near you or start your own. Mini Echoes for quick moments; larger meetups when you want more.

Meet & Remember

Show up, connect, and let your Echo Memory grow — a reflection of moments, not metrics.

Small Moments. Big Impact.

Mini Echoes are small, spontaneous meetups — a morning jog, a study break, or coffee nearby. Get a gentle evening ping for tomorrow's plan, or join something starting soon. Connection doesn't need a weekend — it needs a moment.

Safety. Authenticity. Trust.

  • Verified profiles through university or approved ID.
  • Event locations unlock only when enough trusted users join.
  • No likes. No followers. Just presence.
